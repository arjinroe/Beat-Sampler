Beat Sampler BETA

By Arjin Roe

Version: 0.9

This site plays 18 separate drum samples in two selectable banks. Connects to a generic MIDI keyboard, has 2 banks of drums, now mapped for Aruba but cannot change mappings, does not have gain knob.

Mapping for Keyboard:

Kick 1 - C4 (60)

Kick 2 - D4 (62)

Snare 1 - E4 (64)

Snare 2 - F4 (65)

HH Closed - G4 (67)

HH Open - A4 (69)

Tom 1 - B4 (71)

Tom 2 - C5 (72)

Tom 3 - D5 (74)
